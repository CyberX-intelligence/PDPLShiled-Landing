What is PDPL compliance?
The Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) sets requirements for collecting, processing, and storing personal data in Saudi Arabia. Our platform helps organizations meet these requirements.
How does PD-FORT ensure compliance?
PD-FORT provides automated tools for data discovery, classification, consent management, and breach detection - all aligned with PDPL requirements. Our platform continuously monitors compliance and provides actionable insights.

What are the PDPL requirements for data processing?
PDPL requires organizations to obtain consent, implement appropriate security measures, and maintain records of data processing activities. Our platform automates these requirements.
How does breach detection work?
Our system uses AI and pattern recognition to monitor data access patterns and detect potential breaches in real-time, allowing you to respond quickly as required by PDPL.
What are the penalties for PDPL non-compliance?
PDPL violations can result in significant penalties, including fines up to 5 million SAR. Our platform helps prevent violations through proactive compliance monitoring and alerts.
